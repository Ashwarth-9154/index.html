Tribute to Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Biography

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, India, is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national team. Known for his calm demeanor and strategic acumen, he is widely regarded as one of the greatest captains in the history of cricket.

Achievements

  • ICC T20 World Cup Winner (2007)
  • ICC Cricket World Cup Winner (2011)
  • ICC Champions Trophy Winner (2013)
  • Captain of Chennai Super Kings (CSK) in IPL
  • Fastest Indian to score 8000 runs in ODIs
  • Recipient of the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award (2007)
  • Padma Bhushan Award (2009)
